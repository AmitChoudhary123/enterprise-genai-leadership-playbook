# Contributing

This repository is a practical leadership playbook. Contributions should improve clarity, usefulness, or enterprise applicability.

## Good contributions

- Better decision frameworks
- Practical checklists
- Realistic anti-patterns
- Templates that leaders or architects can reuse
- Industry-specific examples without confidential details

## Content standard

Contributions should be concise, original, and grounded in delivery experience. Avoid generic definitions, vendor marketing, and academic filler.

## Review checklist

- Does this help a leader make a better decision?
- Does it include practical guidance or a reusable framework?
- Is it readable by both technical and business stakeholders?
- Does it avoid confidential or client-specific information?
