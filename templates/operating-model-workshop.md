## Participants

- Executive sponsor
- AI/data/platform leader
- Enterprise architect
- Business domain leaders
- Risk/compliance partner
- Product/delivery leads
- Change/adoption lead

## Pre-work

- Current AI use-case inventory
- Known platform capabilities
- Current governance process
- Top 5 business priorities
- Known pain points in delivery and adoption
