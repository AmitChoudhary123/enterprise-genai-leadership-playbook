# Use-Case Prioritization Framework

Score GenAI initiatives across five dimensions.

| Dimension | Score 1 | Score 5 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Business value | Nice-to-have productivity | Material revenue, cost, risk, or experience impact |
| Process readiness | Unclear workflow | Stable, measurable workflow |
| Context readiness | Knowledge scattered and unowned | Approved, structured, owned knowledge |
| Risk manageability | High ambiguity and exposure | Clear controls and accountable owners |
| Adoption feasibility | Many behavior changes | Fits naturally into work |

## Recommendation

Prioritize initiatives with high value, strong context readiness, and manageable risk. Avoid high-risk workflows until evaluation and governance patterns are proven.
